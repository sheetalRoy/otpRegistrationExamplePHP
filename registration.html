about us

INCOTE Foundation is a platform for IT companies of Manipur to initiate and undertake key IT projects in the state and beyond.

INCOTE FOUNDATION was formed by the IT Entrepreneurs of Manipur on 18-Apr-2017 and registered at the ROC, Shillong MCA on 16-03-2018.
